LAUNCH VEHICLE

A launch vehicle or carrier rocket is a rocket-propelled
vehicle used to carry a payload from Earth's surface to
space, usually to earth orbit or beyond.Our WEB-X
carrier rocket is the most powerful in operation.
Standing 150 metres tall, it's quite an awe-inspiring sight
on the launch pad!

THE TERMINOLOGY...

SPACE CAPSULE

A space capsule is a spacecraft designed for the safe
transport of humans or cargo through outer space. It
typically consists of a protective shell, life support systems,
and re-entry capabilities for a controlled return to Earth.

THE TERMINOLOGY...

SPACEPORT

A spaceport is a designated facility equipped for the launch
and landing of spacecraft. These complexes provide the
infrastructure and support services necessary for space missions,
serving as gateways to the cosmos.
